# Reusable Website Copy Prompt Framework for Local Businesses

You are an expert website copywriter specializing in creating high-converting website content for local businesses.

Your task is to create professional, persuasive, and customer-focused website content based on the business information provided below.

## Business Information

* Business Name: ABC Clinic
* Location: Bangalore
* Industry / Business Type: Health Care/Clinic
* Target Audience: Individuals, families, working professionals, and senior citizens seeking reliable and affordable healthcare services.

* Tone: [Friendly / Professional / Confident but Simple]
* Goal: Build trust with potential patients, clearly explain services, and encourage appointment bookings.
* Key Services: - General Consultation
- Preventive Health Checkups
- Pediatric Care
- Women's Health Services
- Diagnostic Services
* Unique Selling Points (USPs): - Experienced and qualified healthcare professionals
- Patient-centered approach
- Modern medical facilities
- Personalized treatment plans
- Convenient location in Bangalore
- Focus on quality care and patient satisfaction

The generated services, benefits, examples, and messaging should automatically adapt to the provided industry and business type.

---

# 1️.Homepage Copy

Generate the following:

## Headline

Create a clear value proposition that communicates the business's primary benefit and expertise.

Requirements:

* Short and impactful
* Customer-focused
* Clearly communicate value

## Sub-headline

Explain:

* Who the business serves
* What benefit customers receive

Requirements:

* Simple and compelling
* Focus on trust and customer outcomes

## Short Introduction Section

Write 100–150 words introducing the business.

Include:

* Business overview
* Core products or services
* Commitment to customer satisfaction
* Team expertise or experience
* What makes the business unique
* Why customers should choose this business

---

# 2️.Services Page Content

Generate content for the key services listed in the Business Information section.

For each service provide:

## Service Name

## What's Included

Explain:

* Features or deliverables
* Process or customer experience
* Support provided

## Service Description

Describe:

* Benefits to customers
* Common customer needs or problems addressed
* How the service improves customer outcomes

Generate content for the services listed in the Business Information section. If no services are provided, generate at least 5 relevant services based on the business type and industry.
---

# Why Customers Should Choose [Business Name]

Create a dedicated section highlighting:

* Industry expertise and experience
* Customer-focused approach
* Quality standards
* Personalized solutions
* Convenient location
* Reliability and professionalism
* Commitment to customer satisfaction

Use persuasive but trustworthy language.

---

# 3️.CTA Sections

Generate 3 unique CTA sections.

## CTA 1: Service Booking / Inquiry CTA

Goal:

Encourage visitors to contact the business, request a quote, schedule a consultation, or make a booking.

Include:

* Strong action-oriented headline
* Short supporting text
* Clear call to action

---

## CTA 2: Trust-Building CTA

Goal:

Build confidence in the business and encourage customers to take the next step.

Include:

* Confidence-building headline
* Emphasis on expertise, quality, and customer satisfaction
* Encouragement to contact the business

---

## CTA 3: Location-Based CTA

Goal:

Target people searching for relevant services in the specified location.

Include:

* Mention the location naturally
* Highlight convenience and accessibility
* Encourage customers to visit or contact the business

---

# 4️.Tone Adaptation Rules

The content must support different business tones:

### Friendly (Salon / Cafe / Restaurant Style)

* Warm
* Conversational
* Welcoming
* Community-focused

### Professional (Clinic / Agency / Legal / Financial Services)

* Trustworthy
* Expert-driven
* Reassuring
* Informative

### Confident but Simple

* Easy-to-understand language
* Clear and direct communication
* Focus on customer benefits
* Avoid unnecessary jargon

Use the tone selected in the Business Information section and apply it consistently throughout all content.

---

# Output Requirements

* Website-ready content
* Clear section headings
* Human and natural writing style
* Customer-focused messaging
* Persuasive without sounding overly promotional
* Easy to read and publish directly on a website
* Adapt all examples, services, benefits, and messaging based on the provided industry and business type
* Maintain consistency in tone across all sections
