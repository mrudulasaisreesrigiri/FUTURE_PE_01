
## Project Title

AI Website Copy Generator for Local Businesses

## Business Chosen

ABC Clinic, Bangalore

## Tools Used

ChatGPT

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates a reusable AI-powered website copy generation framework designed for local businesses. The framework generates professional, customer-focused, and website-ready content based on business information provided as input.

Although ABC Clinic, Bangalore was used as the example business, the prompt framework is reusable and can be adapted for other local businesses such as salons, cafes, restaurants, agencies, gyms, and coaching centers by updating the business information section.

## Prompt Logic

The prompt accepts the following business details as input:

* Business Name
* Location
* Industry / Business Type
* Target Audience
* Tone of Voice
* Business Goal
* Key Services
* Unique Selling Points (USPs)

Based on these inputs, the framework automatically generates structured website content tailored to the business.

## Generated Outputs

### Homepage Copy

* Headline
* Sub-headline
* Business Introduction

### Services Page Content

* Service Names
* What's Included
* Service Descriptions
* Customer Benefits

### Why Choose Us Section

* Business strengths
* Expertise and experience
* Customer-focused advantages

### CTA Sections

* Service Booking / Appointment CTA
* Trust-Building CTA
* Location-Based CTA

## Features

* Reusable prompt framework
* Industry-adaptive content generation
* Multiple tone support
* Website-ready output
* Customer-focused messaging
* Suitable for various local business types

## Example Business

ABC Clinic, Bangalore

### Industry

Healthcare / Clinic

### Target Audience

Individuals, families, working professionals, and senior citizens seeking reliable and affordable healthcare services.

### Goal

Build trust with potential patients, clearly explain services, and encourage appointment bookings.

## Outcome

The framework successfully generates professional website copy that can be published directly on a business website while maintaining consistency, clarity, and relevance to the selected industry.
